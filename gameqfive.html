You are a disappointing star, can you be the first to emit the first gamma ray burst in the Milky way and ruin Chad’s frat party?

You’re on the right track, but I can’t let you advance unless you tell me how scientists discovered they were really looking at GRBs and not something else entirely.

They made the “Beppo-Sax” Satellite in 1991, and the “Compton Gamma Ray Observatory” in 1997. These two hunks of metal that they shot into space made it so that they could understand what they didn’t before about gamma ray bursts, such as why the energy used to create gamma ray bursts wasn’t simply converted into kinetic energy, or how far away these gamma rays were coming by measuring the redshift (2)
