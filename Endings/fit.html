You are a disappointing star, can you be the first to emit the first gamma ray burst in the Milky way and ruin Chad’s frat party?

Through your telekinetic powers, you are able to transfer your mind to a cat on Earth! You channel your rage into the cat, and the cat proceeds to throw a fit in the house it lives in, scratching up the sofa, biting the owners, and slaying any squirrel in sight. Unfortunately, the cat lives in a separate country from Chad, and does not ruin the frat party.
